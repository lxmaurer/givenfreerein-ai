About Given Free Rein

Given Free Rein documents the living bond between people and horses across cultures, landscapes, and time. Our editorial stories explore the traditions, skills, and communities that keep equestrian heritage alive worldwide.

Our Mission

We believe that horses are more than animals—they are partners, teachers, and a living link to history. Through long-form stories, photography, and interviews, we celebrate the art, people, and places that define horse culture.

Contact

For story pitches, collaborations, or to share your own horse culture, email hello@givenfreerein.com.
