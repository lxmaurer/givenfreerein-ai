Given Free Rein is a long-form journal dedicated to exploring horse cultures, traditions, and the enduring relationship between horses and humans across the world. It's a project shaped by genuine curiosity, respect, and a quiet excitement to share. Each piece is crafted to offer depth, clarity, and a sense of understanding — with the aim of helping you see horses, their worlds, and the people connected to them a little more clearly.
Inside the journal
  • Horse cultures and working traditions shaped by landscape and history
  • Breed histories and the regions that define them
  • Riding styles, tack, and the craftsmanship behind them
  • Destinations where horses remain part of everyday life
